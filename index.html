    Packing & Quick Travel Guide

    Weather-based essentials

    • Hot climates: breathable clothing, sunhat, sunscreen, reusable water bottle.
    • Cold climates: layered clothing, insulated jacket, gloves, warm footwear.
    • Wet seasons: lightweight rain jacket, quick-dry clothing, waterproof bag for electronics.

    General must-haves

    • Copies of important documents and a small first-aid kit.
    • Portable charger, universal adapter, and any required medications.
    • Comfortable day bag and secure wallet or money belt for busy areas.

    Local awareness

    Check local customs and dress codes, learn a few basic phrases, and research health or entry requirements before travel. Respect protected areas and follow local guidance to minimize your footprint.

    Quick tips: pack light, choose reusable items, and always carry copies of your documents.
